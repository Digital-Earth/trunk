Our Vision

The Earth is a wealth of information and wonderment that feeds the curiosity in us all. At PYXIS™, we believe we have a responsibility to keep our planet safe through the empowerment of knowledge. Our WorldView™ tools and technologies enable anyone to have the unfettered power to observe and explore the planet's beauty, its diversity and its vulnerability.

Our WorldView.gallery is a collaborative community of explorers and decision-makers looking for answers to their geospatial questions. They want to know "Where in the world is it?" and "What is in that location?", WorldView™ Studio’s Digital Earth interface is the platform where data comes to life.

We hope you enjoy building your own worldview and find it as #GeoAmazing as we do!
